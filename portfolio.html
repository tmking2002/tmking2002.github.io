softballR Package

I created an R package with the purpose of scraping and cleaning NCAA softball data. As of right now, the package can scrape play-by-play, scoreboards, and box scores from the NCAA and ESPN websites as well as pulling current rankings from a few websites including Massey Ratings and USA Today.

NCAA Softball Power Rankings

Using data from my package and a linear regression model, I was able to create a power rankings model that has accurately predicted the winner of over 67% of games this season. The basis of the ratings system is that it gives teams credit for winning games by a larger margin and losing close games (setting it apart from RPI).

NCAA Softball Top Players

Using data scraped from the NCAA website, I accumulated all of the box score data for the entire 2023 season and created my own all-encompassing stats to evaluate hitter and pitcher ability (wOBA and wFIP) that account for strength of schedule.

ACC Softball Tournament Projections

Using data from my package ('load_ncaa_scoreboard') and a model based on my own power rating system, I wrote a program in R that simulates the remainder of the ACC softball season and finds their probabilities to achieve certain thresholds of sucess (top 3, top6, top10, etc.) and their remaining strength of schedule. Player of the Week Predictor Twitter Bot
